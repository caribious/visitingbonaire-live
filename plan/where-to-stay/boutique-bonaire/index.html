Where to stay

Boutique Bonaire Unique Resorts

Boutique Bonaire Unique Resorts is a local collection of eight small-scale resorts dotted around the island — the opposite of a big hotel. Most share the same DNA: magnesium pools, apartment-style comfort, personal hosting, and a location chosen for divers, families or couples. Pick the property that fits your trip; each books on its own page below.

Local tip: several sit within walking distance of Kralendijk — worth knowing if you'd rather not rely on a rental car every day.

The collection

Eight resorts, one island

From the boutique flagship with its own restaurant to intimate dive-group hideaways — each has its own page on the collection's site.

How to choose

  • Divers: Bliss Bonaire, Casita Palma and Kura Kabana sit close to the water and cater to dive groups.
  • Couples & a special trip: Bamboo Bonaire — Jacuzzi rooms, a honeymoon suite and the CHEFS restaurant on site.
  • Families: Blue Bonaire, Pink Lagoon and All Seasons have family rooms and multi-bed apartments.
  • Walk to town: All Seasons, Kura Kabana and Casita Palma are within strolling distance of Kralendijk.

Whichever you pick, everyone entering the water needs the STINAPA Nature Tag, and the reef around Klein Bonaire is a short boat ride from every one of them.

FAQ

What is Boutique Bonaire Unique Resorts?

A local collection of eight small-scale boutique resorts across Bonaire — All Seasons, Bamboo, Bliss, Blue Bonaire, Kura Kabana, Pink Lagoon, Casita Palma and Vista Alegre — offering apartment-style stays with personal service rather than a big-resort experience.

Which Boutique Bonaire resort is best for divers?

Bliss Bonaire, Casita Palma and Kura Kabana are geared to divers and dive groups, close to the water. Book on each resort's own page.

Are the Boutique Bonaire resorts near Kralendijk?

Several are — All Seasons, Kura Kabana and Casita Palma are within walking distance of town. Others sit near the beach, the lagoon or the water-sports coast.
