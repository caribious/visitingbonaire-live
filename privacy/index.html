Privacy

This site keeps things as simple as the island it describes.

visitingbonaire.com is a static website. There are no user accounts, no logins, no newsletter forms and no comment sections. The site itself does not set tracking cookies and does not build profiles of its visitors. You can read every page here without telling us who you are, and we like it that way.

When you book a tour or activity through this site, the booking itself is handled by our island booking partner, Caribious / Activities Bonaire — the local platform that works directly with the tour operators featured here. The checkout process collects the details needed to complete your booking (such as your name, contact details and payment information) and is governed by the booking partner's own privacy terms, which are shown during checkout. We recommend reviewing them before you complete a booking.

Our hosting provider may keep standard technical server logs (such as IP addresses and request times) for security and operational purposes, as virtually all web hosting does. We do not use these logs to identify or track individual visitors.

Questions about this site or about a booking? Get in touch via activitiesbonaire.com, where you'll find current contact details for the booking team.
